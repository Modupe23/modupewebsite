About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student passionate about technology, innovation, and inclusivity. With experience in hardware, software, and web development, I enjoy solving complex problems and creating impactful solutions.

As a Peer Educator and Makerspace Assistant, I guide students in STEM courses and provide hands-on support with soldering, 3D printing, and fabrication. I have built and programmed robotic systems, designed digital circuits, and developed interactive web apps.

During my internship at EDP Renewables North America, I worked on telemetry validation, control system testing, automation scripting, and plant control simulation, gaining valuable experience in renewable energy technology and collaboration.

As a Senator for the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE), I promote student engagement and diversity initiatives while developing strong communication and leadership skills.

I am driven to keep learning, innovating, and advocating for women in STEM to help close the digital divide.
